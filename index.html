Logging UX

Mechanical vs Potting Failure

1
Start each rep with a clean expectation.
Only the clean button advances a streak. This keeps the app aligned with form-over-result.
2
Use Potting Failure for result errors only.
Misses, speed losses, and pattern choices reset the streak but do not reduce focus score unless you also self-flag mechanics.
3
Use Mechanical Self-Flag even on a made ball.
Select the reason first, then self-flag. That rep is logged as a failure, focus score drops, and three consecutive failures trigger the audit prompt.
